How to Use This System

01

Enter Parameters

Navigate to the Recommendation page and enter your field's environmental details: temperature, humidity, rainfall, and soil pH.

02

Submit Data

Click on the "Recommend Crop" button to process your data through our intelligent algorithm.

03

View Results

The system will provide up to 3 crop suggestions with brief explanations of why they are suitable for your field.

Note: The recommendations are based on data analysis and may vary with real-world conditions. Always consult with local agricultural experts for critical decisions.
